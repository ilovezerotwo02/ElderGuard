📚 Know Your Scams

⚠️ Common Scams

  • Fake cops: Someone calls saying they're from the police, you're in trouble, and you need to transfer money to a "safe account"
  • Fake customer service: "We're from Taobao/JD, your order has an issue, click this link for a refund"
  • Get-rich-quick: Promises of huge returns on investments that sound too good to be true
  • Fake jobs: "Earn money by completing tasks!" — they take your deposit and disappear
  • Fake friends: Someone pretends to be your child or a relative asking for money urgently

🛡️ Remember

  • Don't trust calls or texts from strangers
  • Don't click on links you weren't expecting
  • Never send money to someone you haven't met in person
  • Never give out your bank card number or SMS verification codes
  • When in doubt, call 110
